Jake GambinWho?

Great question! I'm a hard-working guy looking for a career change. After working almost a decade in environmental services, getting my Associate's Degree, and spending the better part of two years devouring all the books I could, it is time for me to bring all the value I can to the market.

Considering the fact you are on this page, I assume you want to know about me. While I could start back over twenty years ago, I don't think my potty training is relevant.

There are three things that I believe are relevant. The first is my dedication to personal development.

The second is my marketing knowledge. I view marketing as a part of everyday life. If we are asking someone on a date, we need to put our best foot forward. If we want to make a sale for our business, we need to make sure that our materials are catered to that specific client's needs. If we are developing an application or website, it needs to be catered to the user.

The third is my experience about web development.
